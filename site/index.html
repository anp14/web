ABOUT US

The club carries forward its vision and ideas by continuously creating hotspots where pool of technical knowledge meets via workshops and an annually organized event ROBOMANIA where the club members present events to the students which require plethora of practical implementation of concepts.

EVENTS

It comprises evants such as Robowar,Laser Strike, Electro Tekken, Online Event,Bomb Diffusion, Electronic Chess, Electronic art, Reflex, Circuit Wizard, Sherlocked and Electro NFS.

SPONSOR

It is sponsored by Pizza Point, Radio Gorakhpur 90.8, Ridezzup, Engineer's Circle, Hotel Dragati and Black Horse, Royal Enfield, LIC, Domino's Pizza, Sri Ram Jwellers

THE TEAM

The team comprises of students of final year,third year and second year of B.tech and faculty members Of Madan Mohan Malviya University of Technology Gorakhpur.
